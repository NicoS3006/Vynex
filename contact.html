10 to 14 tiles are typically installed per square metre depending on the contour of the roof, with an average of 12. Feel free to contact our team for clarification if you're unsure.

Based off the performance data of our 2023 solar roof tiles, the Vynex system achieves between 100 to 140 watts per square metre.

East, west, or south-facing roofs are ideal for Vynex solar systems, just as with traditional solar panels. A roof angle of no more than 45° is preferred for east and west orientations to capture the most sunlight possible. South-facing roofs produce a midday peak, however typically provide energy when it’s least needed. North facing roofs can still accommodate Vynex solar tiles as long as their pitch is sufficiently low.

As with every modern solar system comprised of crystalline structure cells, the relevant data is used as an estimate. Accordingly, we assume that a Vynex solar cell loses a maximum of 0.5% of power per year. The technical term is either performance decay or power output degradation.
